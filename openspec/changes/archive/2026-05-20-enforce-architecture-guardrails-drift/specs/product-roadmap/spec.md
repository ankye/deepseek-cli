## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Roadmap Evidence Alignment / Roadmap 证据对齐

Product roadmap entries SHALL align maturity labels with package manifests, source evidence, diagnostics, tests, or OpenSpec records.

产品路线图条目必须让成熟度标签与 package manifests、source evidence、diagnostics、tests 或 OpenSpec records 对齐。

#### Scenario: Roadmap claim cites evidence / Roadmap 声明引用证据

- **WHEN** roadmap labels a risk-bearing capability as `implemented` or ready for promotion
- **THEN** it cites evidence that supports the label or records the capability as gated, deferred, placeholder, or unsupported
- **中文** 当 roadmap 将有风险能力标记为 `implemented` 或 ready for promotion 时，必须引用支撑该标签的证据，或将能力记录为 gated、deferred、placeholder 或 unsupported。
